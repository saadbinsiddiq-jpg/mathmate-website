Short Description (50 words)

MathMate is a free AI-powered math learning app for K-12 students. Students snap a photo of any math problem and receive a clear, step-by-step explanation that teaches the method — not just the answer. Available in English, French, and Arabic on iOS and Android.

Last updated: June 2026

Extended Description (100 words)

MathMate is an AI-powered math tutoring app designed to make every student feel capable in math. By pointing their camera at any problem — from elementary arithmetic to high school calculus — students receive instant, step-by-step explanations written in plain English (or French or Arabic), focused on building genuine understanding rather than providing shortcuts. MathMate also includes Quiz Mode for self-testing, a Saved Gallery for revision, streak-based motivation, and progress tracking. The app is free to download and use, available on iOS and Android, and built with child safety and privacy as core principles from day one.

Last updated: June 2026
